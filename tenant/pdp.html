1. Core Problem We Solve

Schools today face significant challenges in managing their data infrastructure. The following pain points are common across educational institutions:

Critical Issues Identified:

  • Fragmented Databases: Disconnected systems across departments (academics, finance, admin) creating data silos
  • Manual Data Entry: Error-prone processes leading to inefficiencies and inconsistencies
  • No Reliable Backups: Risk of losing years of student records due to inadequate systems
  • Compliance Headaches: Struggling to meet GDPR/FERPA regulations with manual processes
  • Limited Insights: Valuable data trapped in spreadsheets, unable to generate actionable insights
